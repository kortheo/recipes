Ingredients

	1. [X]2 cups yellow gram lentils (see note)
	2. [X]2 packed cups of fresh spinach, chopped (or use ½ cup frozen spinach)
	3. [X]½ cup finely chopped onion
	4. [X]2 teaspoon ginger garlic paste (3-4 cloves garlic & 1 inch ginger, grated)
	5. [X]1 teaspoon cumin seeds
	6. [X]½ teaspoon ground turmeric
	7. [X]½ teaspoon ground red chili or Cayenne pepper
	8. [X]½ teaspoon ground coriander
	9. [X]½ teaspoon ground cumin
	10. [X]½ teaspoon garam masala (adds great flavor but can be left out if unavailable)
	11. [X]2 ½ teaspoon salt
	12. [X]¼ cup chopped tomatoes
	13. [X]2 tablespoons lime/lemon juice
	14. [X]Also get greek yogurt
	15. [X]Fruit
	16. [X]Soy milk
	17. [X]Cheap wine if under 5$
	18. [X]Asparagus If cheap
	19. [X]cheap meat if any

Directions

	1. Wash and drain the lentils. Add them to a slow cooker insert with 4 cups of water.
	2. Mix in the spinach, onions, ginger and garlic, all the spices (except garam masala which gets added later,) salt and tomatoes. Cover and cook the lentil and spinach preparation in a slow cooker on a high setting for 4-5 hours or low setting for 8 hours.
	3. Once cooked, sprinkle garam masala (if using) and add the lime/lemon juice to the Daal. Check for salt, adding more if desired.
